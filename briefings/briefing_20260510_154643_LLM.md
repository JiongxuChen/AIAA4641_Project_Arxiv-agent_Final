## Highlighted Papers

### Most Relevant Paper
**When No Benchmark Exists: Validating Comparative LLM Safety Scoring Without Ground-Truth Labels**
Many deployments must compare candidate language models for safety before a labeled benchmark exists for the relevant language, sector, or regulatory regime. We formalize this setting as benchmarkless comparative safety scoring and specify the contract under which a scenario-based audit can be interpreted as deployment evidence.
