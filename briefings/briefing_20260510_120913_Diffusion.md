## Highlighted Papers

### Most Relevant Paper
**Diverse Sampling in Diffusion Models with Marginal Preserving Particle Guidance**
We present EDDY (Exact-marginal Diversification via Divergence-free dYnamics), a guidance mechanism for diffusion and flow matching models that promotes diversity among samples generated while maintaining quality. EDDY exploits symmetries of the Fokker-Planck equation, using drift perturbations that change particle trajectories while preserving the evolving marginal distribution.
