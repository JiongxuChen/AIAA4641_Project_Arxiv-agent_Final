## Highlighted Papers

### Most Relevant Paper
**Why Global LLM Leaderboards Are Misleading: Small Portfolios for Heterogeneous Supervised ML**
Ranking LLMs via pairwise human feedback underpins current leaderboards for open-ended tasks, such as creative writing and problem-solving. We analyze ~89K comparisons in 116 languages from 52 LLMs from Arena, and show that the best-fit global Bradley-Terry (BT) ranking is misleading.
