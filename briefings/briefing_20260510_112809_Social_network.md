### Most Relevant Paper
**Modeling Dependency-Propagated Ecosystem Impact of Changes in Maintenance Activities: Evaluating Support Strategies in the PyPI Network**
Background: Open source software ecosystems exhibit dense dependency networks in which maintenance degradation of structurally central packages can propagate widely. Despite increasing attention to open source sustainability, existing support mechanisms lack an explicit, dependencyaware notion of ecosystem-level impact to guide support decisions.
